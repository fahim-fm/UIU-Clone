About UIU

United International University is established with the generous support and patronage of the United Group, a very successful conglomerate operating in diverse business areas in Bangladesh.

United International University is a private university approved by the Government of the People's Republic of Bangladesh and University Grants Commission (UGC) of Bangladesh.

General Information

Vision

The vision of UIU is to become the center of excellence in teaching, learning and research in the South Asian region.

Mission

The mission of UIU is to create excellent human resources with intellectual, creative, technical, moral and practical skills to serve community, industry and region.
