UIU Scholarship, Tuition and Other Fees, Waiver Policy

UIU provides about 10 to 12 crore taka as scholarship and tuition fee waiver every year, one of the highest amongst the private universities. If you are a committed student, take the challenge and build your future with UIU. Find out the UIU Scholarships, Tuition and Other Fees, Waiver Policy for Undergraduate and Graduate Programs.

Learn More About Detailed UIU Scholarship, Tuition And Other Fees And Waiver Policy

Admission and other fees (Included in the Above Fees):

  • Admission Fee Tk. 20,000/- (non-refundable) as down payment during admission.
  • Caution money for ID Card Tk. 2,000/- (refundable) payable at the time of taking Admission.
  • Per Trimester Fee: Tk. 6,000/- and Lab Fee: Tk. 975/-

Collection of Admission Form

Admission Form is available at UIU Admission Office (Working days: Saturday to Wednesday between 9.00 AM to 4.00 PM). One-time payment of Tk. 1,000/- (non-refundable) for one school. An additional fee of Tk. 500/- will be charged for each extra school.
