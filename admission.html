Programs

Undergraduate Programs

In 4 different schools, UIU offers a total of 12 undergraduate programs. BBA, BBA in AIS, Economics, Civil Engineering, CSE, EEE, Data Science, EEE, Environment and Development Studies (EDS), Media Studies and Journalism (MSJ), English, Pharmacy and Biotechnology & Genetic Engineering.

Graduate Programs

In 3 different schools, UIU offers a total of 5 graduate programs. The list of programs are MBA, EMBA, MSc in CSE, MS in Economics (MSECO), MS in Development Studies (MDS).

List of Scholarships & Waiver

Every Trimester/Semester Top 10% students get 25%-100% tuition fee waiver based on previous results and trimester results. UIU provides about 10 to 12 crore taka as scholarship in every year, one of the highest amongst the private university. If you are a committed student, take the challenge and build your future with UIU.
